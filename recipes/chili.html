Chili to Warm Your Soul

There's nothing better on a chilly winter night than a bowl of hot chili to warm your soul!

Ingredients

  • 3 large jalapenos, chopped
  • 6 large habaneros, chopped
  • 1 large orange bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 large cloves garlic, finely diced
  • 2 poounds ground beef
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans beans
  • chili powder to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, brown ground beef over medium heat, remove from heat and drain.
  2. Drain excess liquid from diced tomatoes.
  3. Drain and rinse beans.
  4. Add all ingredients to a crock pot and set to low for 6-8 hours.
  5. Mix in chili powder to taste.
  6. Ladel into bowls and serve hot.
