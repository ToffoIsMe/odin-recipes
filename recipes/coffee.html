French Press Coffee

Delicious Liquid Energy

Long night? Early morning? Get caffeinated!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tablespoons high quality ground coffee
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Poor water into a kettle, bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Remove water from heat and let sit until temperature drops to 195-200 degrees Fahrenheit/li>
  3. Add ground coffee to frensh press, pour hot water over ground coffee.
  4. Cover and let sit for 1 minute. After 1 minute, stir coffee.
  5. Allow coffee to steep, covered, for 3 to 4 more minutes.
  6. Slowly press plunger down. Pour into mugs and serve hot.
